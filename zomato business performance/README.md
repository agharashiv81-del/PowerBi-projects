
# 🍽️ Zomato Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Zomato Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze food delivery performance, customer behavior, restaurant sales, and operational efficiency. The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics and gain valuable insights through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall food delivery performance.
* Monitor sales and order trends.
* Identify top-performing restaurants and cuisines.
* Evaluate delivery partner performance.
* Understand customer payment preferences.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains food delivery transaction records with the following fields:

* Order ID
* Customer Name
* Customer Location
* Restaurant Name
* Cuisine
* Order Date
* Delivery Time (Minutes)
* Order Amount
* Delivery Partner
* Payment Method
* Rating
* Restaurant Location

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Orders
* Total Sales
* Average Rating
* Average Delivery Time

### 🔹 Interactive Slicers

* Year
* Cuisine
* Payment Method
* Discount Applied
* Customer Location

### 🔹 Visualizations

* 📈 Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
* 🍽️ Sales by Cuisine (Treemap)
* 🏆 Top 10 Restaurants by Sales (Bar Chart)
* 💳 Payment Method Distribution (Donut Chart)
* 🚴 Average Delivery Time by Partner (Column Chart)
* ⭐ Average Rating by Cuisine (Column Chart)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified the highest revenue-generating cuisines.
