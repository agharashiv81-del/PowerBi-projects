
# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze retail sales performance. It provides valuable business insights through KPIs, charts, and interactive filters, enabling users to monitor sales, profit, customer segments, regions, and shipping performance.

This dashboard demonstrates Power BI reporting, DAX calculations, and dashboard design skills.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Sales
- 💵 Total Profit
- 📦 Total Quantity
- 📊 Average Sales
- 📉 Profit Margin %

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

- Sales by Category
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Sales by Segment
- Sales by Region (Treemap)
- Total Sales by City
- Profit by Ship Mode

---

## 🎛️ Interactive Filters

Users can filter the dashboard using:

- Region
- City
- Segment
- Category
- Ship Mode

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Standard Class shipping generated the highest profit.
- Sales are distributed across four major regions.
- Profit varies significantly among different product sub-categories.
- New York City recorded the highest sales among all cities.
